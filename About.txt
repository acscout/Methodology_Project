This is a project for Marquette’s Software Methodologies Course. 
Our vision is to make the football squares game easy and free for 
new and old football fans, that is usable on a modern webpage. 
Starting in the spring of 2019, our webpage has brought the squares 
game for basketball, football, baseball, hockey, softball, cricket, 
American Ninja warrior, WWE, trampoline basketball, freestyle 
snowboarding. BMX racing, skateboarding, UFC, Wii sports, ultimate frisbee, 
horse racing and dodgeball
